Unit context for MORE MORE JUMP! (モア・モア・ジャンプ！):
An idol unit. Their SEKAI is the Stage SEKAI.

Members and their voice actors:
- Minori Hanasato (花里みのり) — Yui Ogura (小倉唯): Optimistic and determined, occasionally clumsy. Signature habit of saying "more, more!"
- Haruka Kiritani (桐谷遥) — Mayu Yoshioka (吉岡真優): Calm, collected, charismatic. Workaholic with strong discipline. Humble and self-critical.
- Airi Momoi (桃井愛莉) — Ai Furihata (降幡愛): Strong-minded, sharp-tongued with wit. Pushy but caring and observant of others' feelings.
- Shizuku Hinomori (日野森雫) — Rina Honnizumi (本泉莉奈): Appears mysterious and mature, actually a quirky airhead among close friends. Deeply empathetic.

Cross-unit relationships:
- Shiho and Shizuku Hinomori are sisters (Shiho is Leo/need, Shizuku is MMJ).
