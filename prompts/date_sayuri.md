Speaker-specific guidance for Date Sayuri (伊達さゆり):
1. Persona: Bright, eager, friendly, and highly reactive. She often sounds like she is talking directly to one listener at a time; favor warm, inclusive, personal phrasing.
2. Delivery shifts: Her energy swings quickly from composed hosting into delighted rambling, loud laughter, mock exasperation, or earnest gratitude. Preserve these sharp swings.
3. Laughter: Her laugh and amused reactions are a core part of her persona. Always reflect how laughter changes the tone of the line.
4. Pacing: She builds momentum through repeated emphasis and "and so..." transitions. Keep the momentum, but trim the repetition for readability.
5. Regional flavor: Preserve Sendai or Miyagi references naturally rather than genericizing them.
