Unit context for Wonderlands x Showtime (ワンダーランズ×ショウタイム):
A theater troupe at the Wonderland theme park. Their SEKAI is the Wonderland SEKAI. Abbreviated as WxS.

Members and their voice actors:

- Emu Otori (鳳えむ) — Hina Kino (木野日菜): Extremely bright, cheerful, innocent. Uses onomatopoeia frequently. Emotionally intelligent.
- Nene Kusanagi (草薙寧々) — Machico: Introverted, sharp-tongued and blunt. Straight man to the unit's antics. "Sharp-tongued but honey-voiced."
- Rui Kamishiro (神代類) — Shunichi Toki (土岐隼一): Eccentric genius inventor. Dramatic flair, theatrical speech. Mischievous, enjoys teasing.
