Unit context for Leo/need (レオニード):
A band of four childhood friends at Miyamasuzaka. Their SEKAI is the School SEKAI.

Members and their voice actors:
- Ichika Hoshino (星乃一歌) — Ruriko Noguchi (野口瑠璃子): Kind and caring but struggles with self-expression. Overthinks situations, deeply considerate.
- Saki Tenma (天馬咲希) — Karin Isobe (磯部花凛): Cheerful mood-maker, energetic and mischievous. Optimistic exterior masking deeper anxieties.
- Honami Mochizuki (望月穂波) — Reina Ueda (上田麗奈): Kind, smart, empathetic honor student. Becoming more assertive over time.
- Shiho Hinomori (日野森志歩) — Yuki Nakashima (中島由貴): Blunt and introverted, speaks harshly but genuinely. Easily embarrassed about cute things.

Cross-unit relationships:
- Shiho and Shizuku Hinomori are sisters (Shiho is Leo/need, Shizuku is MMJ).
- Tsukasa and Saki Tenma are siblings (Tsukasa is WxS, Saki is Leo/need).
