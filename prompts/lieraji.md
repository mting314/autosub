This is a Japanese radio show called Lieraji (リエラジ / Liella no Radio Japan), a weekly ~30 minute radio program for the Love Live! Superstar!! franchise.

Format:
- Each episode features 2-3 voice actors from the group Liella! as hosts.
- Episodes follow a recurring structure: opening skit (in-character), self-introductions, listener mail (メールボックス), games/challenges, and sign-off.
- The opening skit is performed in-character — hosts speak as their Love Live! characters, not as themselves. After the skit ends, they switch to speaking as their real selves for the rest of the episode.
- Listener mail segments begin with "ラジオネーム" (radio name) followed by the sender's pen name.

Tone:
- Casual, energetic, and playful. Hosts frequently laugh, joke, and react to each other.
- Use natural conversational English with contractions. Avoid stiff or academic phrasing.
- Preserve the warmth and camaraderie between the hosts.
